Constellation

Haven't been lucky enough to have seen all the constellations in the night sky? No problemo! Technites brings to you #Constellations! A virtual sky of 18 beauty-to-the-eye constellations, seen one by one, as shiny as the real stars and a chance to get to know all the major constellations out there and realize how beautiful our world really is.

Bat Race

One, Two, Three and GO! Gear yourselves and your cycles up, quite figuratively and literally for the craziest _stationary cycle race_ ever! Paddle away to light up an LED each in series and complete the Bat shape to win the race ! So, who's going to signal the Batman first this year? Will it be James Gordan, your friend or you?

LED GLOBE

Remember the old school globe from the geography class? Well you may not be able to locate places on this #Led_globe but you will surely wonder how your persistence of vision can create illusion and make you spellbound. Not only that, we present #Propeller_message_displayer, which displays the user defined message using the same principle with a propeller and an array of LEDs

INFINITY MIRROR

Inception was still just 3 levels of dreams. We bring to you something even more mind-boggling.. " #The_Infinity_Mirror " ! A _single mirror_ which mirrors itself repeatedly that it ends up being the most simplest yet most eye-catching illusion ever, Beautifying itself with _colourful LEDs_ to project the magnificent ENGI logo !

LASER LIMBIO

How flexible is your body ?? Ever tried bending yourself through laser beams without triggering them ?? We at Technites bring you #LASER_LIMBO an exhilarating game with lasers where you can have immense fun

PRISMA

You don't take a photograph, you make it" Wondering how? Don't worry, we got you covered! Check out the #LIVE_PRISMA at #TECHNITES this Engi. After all the picture that you take with a camera is the imagination you want to create with reality. Get yourself clicked and see how you transform as you choose from a wide range of filters. So why wait. Be an artist yourself! Turn your photos into amazing artworks.

RUBEN'S TUBE

What could be more entertaining than fire and good music? How about fire that will dance to that music? We at Technites bring you #RUBENS_TUBEthat literally breathes the fire of life into us. We all know that sound waves exist around us constantly, but we never get a chance to actually see them. Unless, that is, you happen to have a Ruben’s Tube - a hole-ridden pipe filled with flammable gas which when lit becomes a row of burners! Watch what causes flames to pulse to a beat

MIDI BAND

"Music - that goes in the ears, straight to the heart" How would it be to swing your hands in air to enkindle music. We at #TECHNITES bring to you #MIDI_BAND, a delightful event to encounter an aesthetically pleasing musical experience. Music in which electronic technology is used to access, generate, explore and configure sound materials. Get ready to be stunned by variety of musical instruments, including an "Air Synth", "Smart Flute", "Laser Harp", "Voice Synthesizer", and "Musical Accelerometer Air Glove" all mapped to LED sync for magnetic experience.

CONTRAPTION

Inspired from the basic principle of conservation of energy during conversion, we at TECHNITES bring to you this ENGI one of the most exhilarating spectacle ever. Get ready to be awed by a ball which goes up and down the tracks forever and ever, by a chain of different energy conversion steps, which trigger the next task leading to the completion of the final one. Witness the ravishing contraption, as the ball glides through a track made of small machines, Archimedes screws, toy trains, blowers, pumps, water, gateways, loads of wood, metal and glue and what not.

DANCE DANCE REVOLUTION

Dancing, in itself, is a delightful experience. What if we add a lot of music, and a whole lot of lights too? Blowing the roof off the place would be an understatement then. We present you Dance Dance Revolution (DDR), the Technites version of a dance simulator. On an Engineer night, there's no other place you'd rather wanna be. So challenge a friend and dance up a storm!

Blitzkrieg

You've been targeted to experience Modern Warfare for real! Technites brings to you Blitzkrieg - a thrilling, niche and high-tech version of Laser Tag. Strategize with your team on a LIVE, ACTUAL battlefield arena to vanquish your opponents.

LED WALL

When we go psychedelic, we go all in. When all the lights in the room dance to our tunes, why must the walls stay still?Here, we mesmerise you by making the walls dance to our beats. Rush over to the technites this engi to marvel at the result of hard-work, imagination and sheer determination. Complete with a six band visual equalizer, and tri-color theme, we bring you... The LED Wall
